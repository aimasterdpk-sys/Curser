Prize Information

  • Prizes will be distributed within 7 days after tournament completion
  • All prizes are subject to verification of team eligibility
  • In case of ties, prize money will be split equally
  • Taxes and fees are the responsibility of the winners

Tournament Rules

  • Each team must consist of exactly 4 players
  • All players must be at least 16 years old
  • Teams must register before the deadline
  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics
  • Players must use their registered Free Fire accounts
  • Matches will be played in Classic mode
  • Tournament format: Single elimination bracket
  • Match duration: 20 minutes maximum
  • Disconnection during match results in automatic loss
  • Teams must be ready 10 minutes before scheduled match time
  • No substitution of players after registration
  • Organizers reserve the right to disqualify teams for rule violations
